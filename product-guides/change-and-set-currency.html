Change and set currency

You can easily set your currency with Dealer to better manage the prices of your products! Especially when you have multiple stores that are targeted to customers from different countries. Please read

Changing currency in Dealar

Go to Dealar - Setting – Shop Setting.

Find the CUR part and select the currency you prefer for your store.

Now you have successfully set the new currency for you store after select.

Currency display in DSers

Notice : The currency of product publish is base on CUR setting.

Go to Product - importList – detail.

Now you know how to change the currency in Dealar and how it can help you better manage the prices of your products.
